How to Open a Bank Account in Slovakia

1

Choose Your Bank

Select a bank that fits your needs. Consider branch locations, English services, fees, and digital banking options.

2

Prepare Documents

You'll typically need: valid passport, proof of address in Slovakia, and proof of legal stay (visa/residence permit).

3

Visit the Branch

Schedule an appointment or visit during opening hours. Major branches in cities usually have English-speaking staff.

4

Complete Application

Fill out forms, provide documents, and make initial deposit if required. Receive your account details and bank card.
